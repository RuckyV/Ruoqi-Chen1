Copyright, Creative Common (CC) Licenses, Fair Use


Describe basic principles of copyright.
The principle of reconciling the interests of the author and the public.
The principle of protecting the rights and interests of authors.
Keep in line with the trend of international copyright development.
Principles that encourage the dissemination of good work.



Creative Commons licenses give everyone from individual creators to large institutions a standardized way to grant the public permission to use their creative work under copyright law.
attribution, BY: The licensee must make his name known to the owner of the work in the proper way. As of version 2.0, all CC licenses require attribution. While other abbreviations for rights are taken from the initials of the English language, signature (BY) comes from the English preposition by (by... Creation).
share-alike (SA) : The official translation is "sharing in the same way", which requires that after the licensee ADAPTS the works, the adapted works must be released under the same license. In other words, the adapted work must inherit the license of the original work. If there is no inheritance (SA), then the adapted work may be licensed under a stricter license, such as from CC BY to CC BY-NC.
non-commercial (NC) : officially translated as "non-commercial use", the licensee can use the work at will, as long as it is not used for commercial purposes.
No Derivative Works (ND) : The licensee may use the work at will, except for no adaptation or mixing of the work.

Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization (SEO) is to improve the ranking of websites based on the principles of search engines and users' search behavior.

Here are 5 ways to improve your site's visibility on search engines:
1. Get regular updates. Cultivate links. High quality and quantity of external links to your site will affect search engine rankings. Reduce link output.

2. Keep the content original and long.

3. Website page optimization. Update the website frequently.

4. Anchor text of included pages to unincluded pages.

5. Actively push articles, and the links of pages not included are displayed on the home page for a period of time. Pages are published online at an earlier time and indexed by major search engines.

Web Performance & Maintenance

Here are five ways to reduce page load time:
1. Compress resources
Compress js,css and image files
2. Reduce the number of http requests
Merge js,css requests, using Sprite diagrams
3. Style first, logic second
The js file is placed at the end of the body tag (Yahoo Optimization guide)
4. Optimize the request speed
Resource requests are retrieved nearby using CDN links, or the server caches the resource
5. Reduce DOM operations
Too much DOM manipulation can cause rearrangements and stress the rendering process

Describe 5 important actions (tasks) to carry out when maintaining a website:
1. Update the website regularly. 2. Respond to user questions in a timely manner. 3. Backup and collate the website information regularly.
4. Periodically back up and maintain database data. 5. Monitor the security of the website. 6. Analyze the access data of the website regularly.

Web security

1. Port penetration
Block unnecessary ports so that when they are closed or changed, unfriendly visitors are like chickens with their heads cut off.

2. Password attacks
It is recommended that the password contain at least 10 characters and contain a combination of digits, uppercase and lowercase letters, and punctuation marks. The maximum validity period of the password should not exceed 30 days. The account lockout period and the account lockout threshold should be set.

3. Network monitoring
To prevent such snooping, we divide the network into vlans and isolate important computers. Then all the important data in the network will be encrypted transmission, and then the use of "honey pot" technology to create a false terminal full of loopholes, seduce the listener lost direction, finally, we also need to use antisniffer tools to regularly implement anti-monitoring network, sniffing the abnormal data in the network.

4.DDoS
You have to do the first level of identity testing; Second, we need to hide critical services behind a separate firewall; Close unnecessary ports and services again. Finally, we need to periodically scan the network master node.

5. System vulnerability
Pay attention to antivirus software and hardware firewall, but also regular, periodic repair software, system, hardware, firewall and other security system patches, in order to prevent a small vulnerability caused irreparable losses.
